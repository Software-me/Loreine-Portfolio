Filipino Cultural Club (PSST) — pictures & videos

Add your photos and videos

  1. Copy image files into assets/psst/images/
  2. Copy video files (for example .mp4) into assets/psst/videos/
  3. Run scripts/generate-psst-media-json.ps1 to auto-generate assets/psst/media.js and assets/psst/media.json from your files.
  4. Refresh this page (use a local server so the gallery can load the list).
