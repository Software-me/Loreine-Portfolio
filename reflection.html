Reflection

Growth through challenge

Managing academics alongside multiple extracurricular commitments can be both rewarding and challenging. After long days of rehearsals and activities, I sometimes find it difficult to maintain the energy needed for studying. At times, I delay completing assignments even when they are manageable.

However, through these experiences, I have learned the importance of perseverance, self-discipline, and continuous improvement. Despite these challenges, I have steadily improved my academic performance and developed a stronger sense of responsibility as a student.
