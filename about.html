About Me

My academic story

My name is Loreine, and I am currently a senior attending Troy High School, a magnet high school known for its rigorous academic programs. As I approach graduation in the coming month, I am excited to reflect on my growth as a student and individual.

Throughout my high school experience, I have been committed to balancing academic excellence with meaningful extracurricular involvement. Being part of a magnet program has challenged me to maintain strong academic performance, as students are required to earn no lower than a “C” grade each semester. This expectation has strengthened my discipline and resilience as a learner.
